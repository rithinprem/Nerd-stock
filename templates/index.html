What We Do?

Our platform aggregates comprehensive data on block & bulk deals executed by FIIs and DIIs. Follow the smart money and discover where the big players are putting their capital.

Why Block & Bulk Deals Matter

  • Block Deals: Single transactions ≥ ₹5 Cr or ≥ 5 lakh shares. They signal strong conviction.
  • Bulk Deals: Cumulative trades ≥ 0.5 % of equity in a day. They highlight institutional interest.
