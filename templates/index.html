Welcome to NERD's STOCK

What We Do ?


Our platform provides comprehensive data on block and bulk deals made in the stock market. These deals are often executed by large financial institutions such as Foreign Institutional Investors (FIIs) and Domestic Institutional Investors (DIIs). By tracking these transactions, you can gain valuable insights into the investment strategies of major market players.

Why Block and Bulk Deals Matter

  • Block Deals: These are large trades of shares (usually more than 5 lakh shares or shares worth more than ₹5 crore) executed through a single transaction at a price that may or may not differ from the current market price. These deals are significant as they indicate confidence in the stock by large investors.

  • Bulk Deals: These involve a minimum of 0.5% of a company's equity shares being traded in a single or multiple transactions within a trading day. These transactions, when reported, signal substantial buying or selling interest from institutional investors.

How Our Site Helps You

Our site aggregates and displays this crucial data in a user-friendly tabular format, enabling you to:
  • Identify Investment Opportunities: By following the investment actions of large institutional investors, you can discover potential stocks to invest in.
  • Understand Market Trends: Gain insights into which sectors and stocks are attracting institutional interest.
  • Make Informed Decisions: Use the detailed information on each deal to support your investment decisions.
